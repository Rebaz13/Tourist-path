History
  • Some key events since the early 20th century.

Some key events since the early 20th century. 1918: Sheikh Mahmoud Barzinji becomes governor of Sulaimaniya under British rule. He and other Kurdish leaders who want Kurdistan to be ruled independently of Baghdad rebel against the British. He is defeated a year later.

GEOGRAPHY & GEOLOGY
  • Area: 40,643 km²
  • Population: 3,757,058

The Kurdistan Region comprises parts of the three governorates of Erbil, Slemani and Duhok. It borders Syria to the West (Rojava), Iran to the East (Rojhalat), and Turkey (Bakur) lying to the North where fertile plains meet the Zagros Mountains.
